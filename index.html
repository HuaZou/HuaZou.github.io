Biography

I (Hua Zou) am a senior bioinformatics analyst at Xbiome Company since Mar 7 2022. My research interests include host-microbiota intersection, machine learning and multi-omics data integration. I have developed the XMAS 2.0 R package for data analysis on microbiota data.
